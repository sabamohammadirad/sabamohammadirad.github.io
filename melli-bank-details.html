Melli Bank Redesign

  • Category: Professional (Freelance)
  • Date: Feb. - March 2019
  • Company: Melli Bank
  • Location: Tehran, Iran
  • Softwares Used: AutoCAD, Revit, Lumion Pro.

Simulation of Melli Bank Redesign Alternative

  • Redesigned alternative of a melli bank branches with the main purpose of keeping the building formation intact and to promote and integrate Iranian patterns and increase the sense of belonging
  • Redesigned the main façade using brickwork ornaments, as the key element of Seljuk architecture & Turquoise tile pattern regarded to the Safavid period
  • Used tisheh stone with a height of 40, in the plinths, as an architectural pattern in the style of German art
  • Paid special attentions to the form, position, size and its organization windows, as an important element in the façade
  • Repeated the external pattern inside the building in a form mirror work incorporated with plaster, as a very important component of Iranian architecture
  • Designed the linear ceiling lights along with the main façade lines and interior walls
  • My Contribution: Collaborated on façade and interior design, developed 3d models, rendered photo realistic images, created work portfolio for presentation
